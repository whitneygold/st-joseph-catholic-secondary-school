About St. Joseph's

Our Foundation

Established in 1979 by the visionary Bishop Emeritus of Ekiti Diocese, Most Rev. Michael O. I. (of blessed memory). St. Joseph's College was built on the pillars of spiritual formation, academic excellence, and community service. Located at the heart of Ajilosun, Ado Ekiti, we have formed generations of leaders guided by the motto "ORRA ET LABORA" (Pray and Work).

⚜️ Ad Multos Annos — To many years! ⚜️

Educational Ethos

We blend Catholic values with modern pedagogy. Our students excel in sciences, arts, and vocational skills, while participating in robust spiritual activities, debates, and sports. We celebrate 45+ years of shaping Ekiti's brightest minds.

Motto: ORRA ET LABORA · AD MULTOS ANNOS

“In 1979, with the help of faithful clergy and laity, we laid the foundation stone of this great institution to provide integral education rooted in Christ. May St. Joseph intercede for every student who passes through these gates.”

— Most Rev. Michael O. I. (Bishop Emeritus, Ekiti Diocese) ✝️

Our Legacy — Ad Multos Annos

St. Joseph's College started as a minor seminary-inspired institution, later becoming a full-fledged secondary school. Through the years, the school has produced priests, doctors, engineers, and public servants. The crest carries the ORRA ET LABORA inscription, a reminder that prayer and hard work lead to true success. Every year, we celebrate "Josephite Day" honoring St. Joseph, our patron, with cultural displays and academic awards.

Location: Ajilosun, along Old Iyin Road, Ado Ekiti — serene environment conducive for learning.

Inter-House Sports & Academics

Annual athletics, quiz competitions, and cultural debates. The Victorious House Shield is awarded each session. Join the spirit of "ORRA ET LABORA" through teamwork and excellence.

School Anthem

"St. Joseph, our guiding light,
In prayer and work we find our might.
ORRA ET LABORA, our joyful cry,
Under the African sky.
Ad Multos Annos, let the bell ring,
Glory to God, our praises sing!"

(Official anthem composed in 1985, revised during the 25th anniversary)

Bishop Emeritus Most Rev. Michael O. I.

A revered shepherd, educator, and founder. Bishop Michael OI envisioned a school where faith meets reason. Under his patronage, St. Joseph's College Ajilosun has grown into a citadel of learning, and his legacy continues to inspire staff and students. He rests in glory, but his vision — "Ad Multos Annos" — echoes.
